# Non-Functional Requirements
* The app shall be secure and authentication is required for all access.
* The app shall be reliable and available 95% of the time.
* The app shall be usable by users aged 13 and above.
* The user sensitive data shall be stored encrypted.
* Users can only access their own or shared data.
* The app shall load main screens within 2-3 seconds.
* The system shall support future feature expansions.
