# Bucket List App
Bucket List is an app that makes building your own bucket list easier, with a user-friendly interface, easy navigation, and the ability to create a shared bucket list with your loved ones.

## Purpose of the Bucket List App:
The purpose of the Bucket List app is to allow you to write down anything you want to do, anytime and anywhere, whenever an idea comes to mind.

For example, imagine you are walking past your bathroom and think, “These lights are terrible, I’ll change them soon.” But “soon” often never comes. Instead, you can immediately open the app and write down this task.

Later, when you are at the store and feel like you’ve forgotten something, you can open the app and see the task, reminding you to grab new lights.

This concept can be applied to anything: a hobby you want to start, an experience you want to try, or a friend or relative you want to visit or grab coffee with. So whenever you have free time or are on vacation and wondering what to do, you can open the app and browse through the things you’ve always wanted to do.

## App Objectives:
* Develop an easy, simple, and straightforward user interface.
* Create a visually appealing UI with a cute design and a pastel grid aesthetic.
